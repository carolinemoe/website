Grow Up!

Github Repo Itch.io Unity download

Still in development...

The player begins as a fish in the ocean. With determination (and distance) the player defeats fellow fish and evolves.

Now a gorilla in the jungle, the player must brawl with ferocious jungle enemies, finally evolving into a well kempt business man.

In this city however, the player is met with a rude awakening upon trying to fight their peer. Maybe they were not so evolved after all...
